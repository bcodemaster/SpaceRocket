Space Rocket

Introduction

Space Rocket is a small side-scroller game where the player controls a Space Rocket, attempting to navigate between columns of grey pipes without hitting them. Play as a Rocket and navigate into this dangerous space adventure game as you try to achieve the highest score by passing through grey pipes!

Controls

Press the "Space Bar" to make your Rocket go up!
